## üìñ How to Use the SuperMarket System

### üë• For Customers

#### **View Products**
1. Click **"View Products"**
2. Browse the product list
3. Use the sorting buttons if needed

#### **View Product Activities**
1. Click **"View Products"**
2. Click on any product in the list
3. Click **"View Activities"**

#### **Shopping**
1. Click **"Shopping Cart"**
2. Enter the **Product ID** and **Quantity**
3. Click **"Add to Cart"**
4. Click **"Purchase"** when ready
5. You'll see a receipt with the total

#### **Add New Product**
1. Login as staff
2. Click **"Add Product to System"**
3. Fill in the product details
4. Click **"Confirm"**

#### **Add or Remove Stock**
1. Login as staff
2. Click **"Modify Stock"**
3. Enter **Product ID** and **Quantity**
4. Click **"Add Stock"** or **"Remove Stock"**

#### **Delete a Product**
1. Login as staff
2. Click **"Delete Product"** (red button)
3. Enter **Product ID**
4. Click **"Search"**
5. Click **"Confirm Delete"**
